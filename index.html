• Iron Man

    Genius, billionaire, playboy, philanthropist. Tony Stark became Iron Man to protect the world with his intellect and courage.

  • The Incredible Hulk

    Dr. Bruce Banner transforms into the mighty Hulk, channeling uncontrollable rage into unstoppable strength.

  • Captain America

    Steve Rogers, the first Avenger, fights for freedom and justice with his unyielding shield and moral compass.

  • Thor

    The God of Thunder wields Mjolnir to protect the realms with unparalleled might and the heart of a warrior.

  • Spider-Man

    Peter Parker swings through the streets of New York, using his great power responsibly to save the day.

  • Hawkeye

    Clint Barton, master archer and skilled marksman, ensures no target escapes his aim in the fight for justice.
